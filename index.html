STAMINA

Stamina is the strength and energy that allow you to sustain physical or mental effort for long periods of time. Increasing your stamina helps you endure discomfort or stress when you're doing an activity. It also reduces fatigue and exhaustion.


ENDURANCE

Endurance means the ability to sustain yourself over a prolonged period of time. It is an important aspect that determines both our physical and mental strength. Being able to endure strict physical exercise and training over a long period of time gives us great fitness and agility, making us very healthy.
